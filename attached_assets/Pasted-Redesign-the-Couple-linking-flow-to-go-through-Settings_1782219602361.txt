Redesign the Couple linking flow to go through Settings instead of the Find screen.

DESIRED FLOW:
1. User goes to Settings → "Relationship Mode" section
2. Two options: 
   - 💫 "Single" (default) → shows Find Vibe tab
   - 💑 "In a Relationship" → triggers couple linking flow
3. When user taps "In a Relationship":
   - Show a modal/screen to search partner by username
   - Send couple request to them
   - Partner gets a notification and sees the request in their Settings → Relationship Mode
   - Partner taps Accept → both are now linked
   - Bottom tab switches from Find Vibe to Couple for both users
